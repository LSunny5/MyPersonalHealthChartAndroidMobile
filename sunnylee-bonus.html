My Personal Health Chart is your very own personal Medical chart!

“When did you last have your TB Booster? What was the date of your surgery?”

My Personal Health Chart was designed for users like you to help keep track of yours as well as your family and other important individual’s medical information such as medical history, family history, immunizations, medications, allergies, insurance, and other notes. When you go to visit the doctor or enroll your child at school, various health related questions are asked and you as the healthcare provider is requested to provide the information on the spot. Instead of having to make up or guess on the dates and times, even the names of surgeries, you can simply open up this app and provide the necessary information to the other healthcare provider. Perhaps in the future, when you are in need of medical aid and there is no one else around, the healthcare provider can access the information instead of keeping you awake to get information on your medical history. Data is inputted by the user and easily accessible and readable after you have logged in.


Main Features:

- Log in to keep information private from those who you wish not to know

- Clear and simple intuitive interface

- Record medical history, family history, immunizations, medications, allergies, insurances, and notes on various individuals

- Easy and fast data entry and editing allowing for entry at anytime

- Multiuser to allow you to track various individual(s)

- Summary of all medical information for each individual

- Backup and restore of information through username and database

- Supports Android Froyo and up

- Languages: English
